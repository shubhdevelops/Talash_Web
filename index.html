About Us

Our Journey

Born in the corridors of National Institute of Technology Raipur, Tlash Crew represents the raw energy and passion of college life. We're not just a band; we're a movement that believes in authentic expression through music.


Our motto "Raw Real Tlash" embodies everything we stand for - unfiltered emotions, genuine connections, and the relentless pursuit of musical excellence. As NITians, we bring technical precision to our artistic expression.
